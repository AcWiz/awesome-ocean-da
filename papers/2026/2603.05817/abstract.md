## 摘要

We present a localized data assimilation (DA) scheme based on the sequential Markov Chain Monte Carlo (SMCMC) technique [Ruzayqat et al., 2024], a provably convergent method for filtering high-dimensional, nonlinear, and potentially non-Gaussian state-space models. Unlike particle filters, which are exact methods for nonlinear non-Gaussian models, SMCMC does not assign weights to samples and therefore avoids weight degeneracy in small-ensemble regimes.
