## 摘要

Accurate characterization of subsurface flow is critical for Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) but remains challenged by the ill-posed nature of inverse problems with sparse observations. We present Function-space Decoupled Diffusion Posterior Sampling (Fun-DDPS), a generative framework that combines function-space diffusion models with differentiable neural operator surrogates for both forward and inverse modeling.
