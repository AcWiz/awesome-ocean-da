## 摘要

Variational data assimilation in ocean models depends on the ability to model general correlation operators in the presence of coastlines. Grid-point filters based on diffusion operators are widely used for this purpose, but come with a computational bottleneck - the costly estimation of normalization factors for every model grid point.
